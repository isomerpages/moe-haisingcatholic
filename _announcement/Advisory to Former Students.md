<h2><strong>Advisory to Former Students: Collection of School Graduation Certificates</strong></h2>
<p>Hai Sing Catholic School would like to inform all former students who
have graduated but have not yet collected their School Graduation Certificate
(SGC) to do so by 31 December 2025. After this date, any uncollected certificates
will be disposed of in accordance with administrative guidelines.</p>
<p>Please visit the General Office during operating hours to collect your
SGC, or authorise a representative to do so on your behalf with the necessary
documents. For enquiries, please contact the General Office at 6582 7864.</p>
