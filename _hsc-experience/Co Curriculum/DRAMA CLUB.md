<h2>Student Voice</h2>
<p>‘Be loud. Be proud. Be you. That’s the motto of the drama club. The motto
has urged me to be myself and never change myself to ‘fit in’. Being in
the drama club has made me realise that we have many hidden talents and
we get to learn the talent we have deep within us. Drama Club has taught
me to appreciate myself through my performances. I often find myself calling
the drama club, a drama family and I am proud to be in our drama family.’_</p>
